 The cow is a domestic animal. It is very gentle in nature. It is very useful animal. It is a four footed animal. It has two ears two eyes two horns and a long tail. Its body is covered with soft and short hair. Cows are of different colours such as red black white etc. The cow is found all over the world. It lives on grass straw oilcake etc. It is very found of its calf. The cow gives us milk. Milk is an ideal food. We get butter ghee cheese etc from milk. Ox and bullocks draw carts and plough the lands. We must take care of our cows.
